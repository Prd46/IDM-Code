(BE ADVISED: This video contains some intense themes and a frame that may be sensitive for some viewers) This was my final animation project for my Design for Media class, the requirements being very simple: just make it mostly black and white and be creative with your designs. I took it as an opportunity to take a deep dive into my emotions again, making something of it to perhaps portray it in a more tangible way. I was in a pretty bad mental state in my first term, feeling the all imposing isolation of Covid restrictions and my fears of inadequacy amongst my peers. By the end though, I was feeling quite a bit better, the quality of this project partially giving me a boost in confidence. Drawn in Firealpaca, animated in Krita.
